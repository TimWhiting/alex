Overview

Alex is a tool for generating lexical analysers in Haskell, given a description of the tokens to be recognised in the form of regular expressions. It is similar to the tool lex or flex for C/C++.

Development

Alex is licensed under a BSD-style license. The current sources are in a Darcs repository, to get the latest version say:

   darcs get http://darcs.haskell.org/alex/

Feel free to hack on Alex and darcs send me your patches.
